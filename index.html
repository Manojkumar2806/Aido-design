Frequently asked questions

How does Aido work?

Aido is a smart speaker device that guides kids step-by-step using audio questions instead of showing answers on a screen. It waits for the student's voice response to decide the next tip.

Is there a subscription?

No. Your one-time purchase covers all standard math, language, and science lessons for grades K-5. Future library content is free.

What age group is it for?

Aido is optimized for kids aged 5 to 11 (Kindergarten to 5th Grade), aligning with standard primary school worksheets.

Does it require internet?

Wi-Fi is required to download new assignments and sync parental reports. Once synced, core tutoring modules work offline.

How do you handle privacy?

All speech processing is done on the local hardware chips. No audio recordings are uploaded or saved in cloud servers.
